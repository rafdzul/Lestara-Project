Illegal Mining Case in Unmul Forest Considered Slow, Civil Lawsuit Prepared

April 11, 2025

Illegal mining has caused significant environmental damage to 3.2 hectares of the Educational Forest at Mulawarman University (Unmul) in East Kalimantan. The incident has raised concerns among academics and environmentalists over the slow response from law enforcement.

Unmul Rector Prof. Abdunnur confirmed that the company involved had no legal permit to operate in the protected academic area. He criticized the lack of urgency from authorities and called for immediate legal action to safeguard the university’s forest assets.

In response, Unmul is preparing to file a civil lawsuit against the responsible party. The case has sparked public calls for stricter enforcement and stronger protection of educational and conservation areas from illegal exploitation.
