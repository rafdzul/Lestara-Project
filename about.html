About Us

Lestara is a non-profit organization focused on environmental conservation and sustainable practices. Our mission is to empower communities to take part in preserving nature.

Our Vision

To create a sustainable and greener future for generations to come.

Our Mission

Empowering communities through education and environmental projects.

Our Goals

  • Raise awareness about environmental issues.
  • Organize community clean-up and tree planting activities.
  • Collaborate with local governments to promote sustainability.
